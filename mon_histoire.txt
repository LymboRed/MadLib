In the glorious kingdom of Talez, a humble hero named Red set out on a quest to Dragon Slay. Armed only with a Roy's Necklace, they had to face the terrifying Achnologia sent by Rolig. Along the way, they met Derik, who gave them a mysterious Dragon Tooth. After a long journey through the village of Earthstone, they saved Hearts and returned home. For their bravery, the citizens of the Realm now call them the Dragon Slayer.
